New to Bachata?

You're exactly who we built this for.

We've seen it all — the two left feet, the "I can't dance" crowd, the first-timers who haven't danced since Year 9 prom. They're all regulars now.

"I don't have a partner."

You won't need one — not for a single second. Our classes rotate so you'll dance with the whole room. Most of our regulars arrived solo.

No partner needed

"I have two left feet."

Everyone in the beginners class started exactly where you are. We teach from absolute zero — no steps assumed. If you can walk, you can Bachata.

Zero experience needed

"What should I wear?"

Casual clothes are fine. Wear something you're comfortable moving in. Flat-soled shoes or trainers work great. No heels needed for beginners.

Come as you are

"Will it be awkward?"

The first five minutes might feel a bit weird — totally normal. By the end of the class, you'll be laughing and wondering why you waited so long.

Everyone was new once

"Am I too unfit / too old?"

We have students from their 20s to their 60s. Bachata is low-impact. The class is an hour; the pace is friendly. You set your own intensity.

All ages, all levels

Your first class is FREE.

No card details, no commitment. Come, see if you like the vibe, meet the crew. If it clicks — brilliant. If not — no hard feelings.

What a typical class looks like

01
Warm welcome
Introductions, a quick warmup, no awkward standing around. Everyone here was new once.
02
Foundation technique
The basic step, timing, how to hold frame — broken down until it clicks, not just demonstrated once.
03
Rotating partners
You'll dance with everyone in the room. By the end, you'll have ten new mates.
04
Social freestyle
Class ends, the music stays on. This is where the real magic happens.

No booking required — just turn up. We recommend sending a quick WhatsApp message first so we know to expect you. But every class is walk-in.

Bachata is a Latin partner dance from the Dominican Republic — think sensual movement, rhythmic footwork, and a close connection with your partner. The music is melodic and emotional, and the dance style is warm and expressive. It's one of the fastest-growing social dances in the UK.

You can dance at the free social party after your very first class. Most people feel comfortable dancing socially after 4–6 weeks. The 4-Week Newcomer Pass is designed exactly for this transition.

Our community spans 20s to 60s. We have students, professionals, retirees, parents — everyone in between. The vibe is warm and inclusive. Latin dance makes age completely irrelevant.
