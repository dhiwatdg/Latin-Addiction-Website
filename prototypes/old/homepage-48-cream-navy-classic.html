Welcome

New to Bachata?

Everyone starts somewhere. We make your first steps easy, welcoming, and genuinely fun.

No Partner Needed

We rotate partners throughout every class so you'll dance with everyone and learn to lead or follow naturally. Come alone or bring friends — either way, you're covered.

No Experience Needed

Every one of our students started as a complete beginner. Our classes build from the ground up, step by step, in a relaxed and supportive atmosphere — no pressure, ever.

First Class Free

No commitment, no pressure. Just show up to any of our weekly classes and experience Bachata for free. If you love it (you will), you can sign up afterwards.
