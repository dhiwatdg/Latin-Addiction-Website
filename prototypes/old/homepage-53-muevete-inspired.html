Our Promise

Everyone is welcome here —
no exceptions

Latin Addiction UK is built on the belief that dance belongs to everyone. Whether you're 18 or 80, total beginner or returning dancer — you'll be met with warmth, patience, and a community that genuinely has your back.

🤝

No Partner Needed

Come solo, leave with a full dance card. We rotate partners every few minutes so you meet everyone.

🌱

Zero Experience

Our beginners class is specifically designed for people who have never danced before — ever.

🌍

All Backgrounds

Our rooms reflect the full diversity of our cities. There's no "typical" dancer here — just people who love to move.

💛

Judgment-Free

We all started somewhere. The only expectation is that you turn up and give it a go.

What to expect on your first night

1
Show up, no preparation needed
Wear something comfortable you can move in. Flat, smooth-soled shoes are ideal but not required.
2
We start from absolute zero
The beginner class assumes nothing. Every move is explained from scratch, clearly and patiently.
3
Rotate partners every few minutes
You'll practise with everyone in the room. It's a great way to meet people and improve faster.
4
Stay for the free social party
After class, the floor opens for free social dancing. The best part of the night — don't skip it.
