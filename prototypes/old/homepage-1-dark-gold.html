Welcome

New to Bachata?

Everyone starts somewhere. We make your first steps easy, welcoming, and genuinely fun.

No Partner Needed

We rotate partners throughout every class, so you'll dance with everyone and learn to lead or follow naturally. Come on your own or bring friends — either way, you're covered.

No Experience Needed

Every single one of our students started as a complete beginner. Our classes build from the ground up, step by step, in a relaxed and supportive atmosphere.

First Class FREE

No commitment, no pressure. Just show up to any of our weekly classes and try it for free. If you love it (you will), you can sign up afterwards.
