Our Story

Where Bachata
Becomes Belonging

Latin Addiction was born out of a simple belief — that dance is not a talent you're born with, it's a feeling you discover. We built a school where total beginners are welcomed with the same warmth as experienced dancers.

Bachata is more than steps. It's connection — with the music, with a partner, and with yourself. Our classes are small, personal, and infused with the Latin spirit that makes this dance so irresistible.

Based across Reading and Leicester, we've grown quietly — one class, one dancer, one unforgettable evening at a time.

Dancing is the closest thing to magic — it transforms a room, a mood, a life.
