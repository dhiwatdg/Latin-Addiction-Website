First-Timers

Built for people
who've never danced.

Every single thing on this page is designed for the person who's standing at the door wondering if they should come in. Yes, you should.

"I don't have a partner."

Most people come alone. We rotate partners throughout the class — you'll dance with everyone. It's the best way to improve fast and it's genuinely how everyone does it.

"I have two left feet."

Everyone in the beginners class started exactly where you are. We teach from absolute zero — no steps assumed, no experience required. If you can walk, you can Bachata.

"What should I wear?"

Casual clothes are completely fine. Wear something you're comfortable moving in. Flat-soled shoes (trainers are great) or dance shoes work well. No heels needed for beginners.

"Will it be awkward?"

Honestly? The first five minutes might feel a bit weird — that's totally normal. By the end of the class, you'll be laughing and wondering why you waited so long to try it.

"Am I too unfit / too old?"

We have students from their 20s to their 60s, all fitness levels. Bachata is low-impact to learn. The class is an hour; the pace is friendly. You set your own intensity.

Your first class is free.

No card details upfront. No commitment. Turn up, try it, love it — then decide. If it's not for you, no hard feelings and no cost.
