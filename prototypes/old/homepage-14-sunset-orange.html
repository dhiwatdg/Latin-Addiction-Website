The curriculum

What you'll pick up
from day one

We teach modern bachata — sensual, musical, and genuinely fun to do.

Rhythm & Timing

Feeling the 1-2-3, 5-6-7 bachata beat. Even if you think you have no rhythm — you do.

Partner Work

Lead and follow techniques. How to connect with a partner and communicate through movement.

Footwork Patterns

Basic steps, side steps, turns, and styling. Building blocks that unlock everything else.

Body Movement

The sensual wave, body rolls, and hip movement that give bachata its signature feel.

Styling & Expression

Adding your personality to the dance. How to look good, feel confident, and enjoy every song.

Social Dancing

How to ask someone to dance, dance socially at events, and be a great partner on the floor.

Common questions

Got a question?
We've got answers

Do I need to bring a partner?
Not at all. We rotate partners throughout the class so everyone gets to dance with everyone. It's actually one of the best parts — you meet new people every session.
What should I wear?
Anything comfortable you can move freely in. Avoid flip-flops — trainers or shoes with a smooth sole work best. No special dance shoes required when you're starting out.
How much do classes cost?
Your first class is completely free — no strings attached. After that, prices are kept affordable. Message us on WhatsApp for current rates.
Is there an age limit?
Classes are for adults (18+). We have a wide range of ages in each class — from students to retirees. Everyone is welcome.
Can I join mid-term or do I need to start at the beginning?
Drop in any week — there's no rigid term structure. Beginners are taught the foundations every session, so you'll never feel like you've missed something important.
What style of bachata do you teach?
We teach modern bachata, blending sensual and traditional styles. Think beautiful body movement, musicality, and connection — approachable for all levels.
