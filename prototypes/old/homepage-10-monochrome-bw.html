01 The Basics

NEW TO
BACHATA?

Bachata is a sensual, rhythmic dance from the Dominican Republic. Easy to start, deeply satisfying to master. You don't need any experience — or a partner.

COME SOLO

No partner? No problem. We rotate partners throughout the class so everyone gets to dance and no one sits out. We've all been the new person.

ZERO EXPERIENCE

Our beginner classes start from absolute scratch — footwork, rhythm, timing. Clear instruction, no judgement, plenty of laughter. You'll be moving by the end of your first hour.

FIRST CLASS FREE

Try us before you commit. Your first session costs nothing. Turn up, dance, see if you love it. Wear comfortable clothes and flat shoes — that's all you need.
