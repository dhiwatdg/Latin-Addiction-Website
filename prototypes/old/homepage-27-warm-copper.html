Welcome

New to Bachata?

Everyone starts somewhere. We make your first steps easy, welcoming, and genuinely fun.

No Partner Needed

We rotate partners throughout every class, so you'll dance with everyone and learn to lead or follow naturally. Come solo or bring friends — either way, you're covered.

No Experience Needed

Every student here started as a complete beginner. Our classes build from the ground up, step by step, in a relaxed and supportive atmosphere — no judgement, ever.

First Class FREE

No commitment, no pressure. Show up to any weekly class and try it for free. If you love it — and you will — you can sign up afterwards.
