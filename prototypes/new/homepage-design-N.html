Why Bachata

Why people fall in love with Bachata

01

A simple 4-count rhythm

Side-to-side steps that let you focus on your partner, not your feet. If you can count to four, you're ready.

02

Music you'll actually connect with

Guitar-driven, melodic, and emotional. You'll feel the rhythm before you even step onto the floor.

03

Dance socially from night one

You'll actually dance at the social party the same night you learn your first steps. Not months from now. Tonight.

04

Connection over choreography

Bachata is about how it feels between partners, not flashy footwork. Built for feeling, not performing.

We teach both lead and follow — you choose which role suits you.
