Your Instructors

People who actually care if you have fun

Dhiwa & Gloria · Founders

Latin Addiction was founded by Dhiwa and Gloria — two people who built a teaching method around technique, safety, and genuine fun.

Gloria discovered Bachata in 2019. She was once exactly where you are — no dance experience, no idea what to expect, nervous about walking through the door. That perspective shapes everything about how we teach.

Together, they teach and DJ at festivals across the UK, and run classes in Milton Keynes, Leicester, and Reading.

FAQ

The questions everyone asks first

Comfortable clothes you can move in — jeans are fine. Wear clean, smooth-soled shoes if you have them (trainers work too, just avoid anything that grips the floor). No special dance shoes needed.

Honestly? The first five minutes might feel a bit new. But the class is structured so you're moving and laughing within minutes. Everyone remembers being a beginner — the room is on your side.

Both are Latin partner dances, but they feel very different. Salsa is faster and more energetic with complex footwork. Bachata is slower, more intimate, built on a simple side-to-side rhythm. It's widely considered one of the most beginner-friendly Latin dances — you can be dancing socially after one class. We teach both Salsa and Bachata in Milton Keynes, and Bachata in Leicester and Reading.

You can turn up on the night, but we'd love a heads-up. Send us a WhatsApp message and we'll be expecting you — it also means we can answer any questions before you arrive.
